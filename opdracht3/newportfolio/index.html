Ik ben in 2011 verhuisd naar Amsterdam om een baan te kunnen vinden en om een betere toekomst te kunnen hebben. I immediately falled in love of this beautiful city. But we have to make a step backward. I grew up in Albenga a little city situated in the Palm Riviera in Liguria. After finishing high school in 2011 I decided to change my life and to step into a new adventure coming living here. It was a new country, a new language and a new way of life. For the first year it was not easy to live alone without family and friends but after a while I started to speaking the language and meeting new people. After some years of hard work in catering I decided to start my new school career in the Netherlands. First i attended an official dutch language course at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam and then I started to study at Communication and Multimedia Design in Amsterdam.
And Now? Now I am at my second year of CMD. Here I've found the perfect course that challenge me everyday doing my best for my future.
